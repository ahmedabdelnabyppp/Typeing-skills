WHAT IS Typeing skills?

Typeing skills is the most effective way to learn how to type.

It is web based and highly effective. Typeing skills are important (and will always be) free for both individuals and schools. There is an optional paid school edition.

Information on the level of app

Typeing skills provides for each group of level you should choose between them .

Easy Level
The easy level you should get at least 60 words per second
Normal Level
The Normal level you should get at least 120 words per second and no error
Hard Level
The Hard level you should get at least 220 words per second and no error
Very hard Level
The Hard level you should get at least 500 words per second and no error
